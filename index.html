Social Media Graphics

Using Adobe Photoshop and InDesign, I've made many graphics for various clubs and groups I've participated in. At the Berkeley Center for Law and Technology, I've designed the 2019 Student Handbook, edited photos used on the official Facebook page, and flyers promoting events sponsored by local law firms.

Portrait Photography

Photography has been a hobby since childhood. In my free time, I take portrait photography for friends, family, and clients. I’m also proficient at Adobe Photoshop, which I use to edit my photos.

Art

I love exploring the reciprocal relationship we share with our environments, how they can influence our lives and how we in turn influence our world. I use oils, acrylics, printmaking, and digital mediums (Adobe Photoshop, Paint Tool SAI).
